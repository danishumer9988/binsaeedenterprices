Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Here's how our return process works:

  • 14-day return window: From date of delivery for most products
  • Condition requirements: Products must be unopened in original packaging with all seals intact
  • Return authorization: Contact us at returns@binsaeed.com for a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  • Shipping costs: Customer responsible for return shipping unless item is defective or incorrect
  • Processing time: Returns typically processed within 3 business days of receipt

Refund Process

Our refund process is designed to be simple and transparent:

  • Processing time: 3-5 business days after we receive your return
  • Method: Refunded to original payment method
  • Bank processing: Additional 3-5 business days may be required by your bank
  • Non-refundable: Original shipping charges and gift wrapping fees
  • Partial refunds: May be issued for items not in original condition

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on:

  • Opened consumables: Any spice products with broken seals
  • Custom orders: Products made to your specifications
  • Third-party items: Products not sold directly by Binsaeed Enterprises
  • Digital products: Downloadable content or gift cards
  • Final sale items: Clearly marked as non-returnable

Damaged or Defective Items

We take quality seriously. If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Notification window: Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Evidence required: Photos of damaged packaging and product
  • Resolution options: Replacement or full refund at your discretion
  • Return shipping: We provide prepaid return labels for defective items
  • Expedited processing: Priority handling for quality issues

For all return inquiries, please contact our customer service team at returns@binsaeed.com with your order number and details.
