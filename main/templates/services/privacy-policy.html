Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect personal information to provide and improve our services. This information is gathered when you:

  • Create an account or place an order on our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Contact our customer service team via email, phone, or chat
  • Participate in surveys, promotions, or contests
  • Interact with our social media platforms

The types of personal data we collect include:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, date of birth
  • Contact Data: Email, phone number, shipping address
  • Financial Data: Payment card details (processed securely)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your purchases
  • Customer Service: To respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our products and user experience
  • Marketing: To send promotional offers (only with your consent)
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Information Sharing

We may share your information with trusted third parties only when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Shipping carriers, payment processors, and IT support
  • Business Partners: Only for direct service fulfillment
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services we request.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login information
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Enable shopping cart functionality
  • Deliver targeted advertisements (with consent)
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

We also use Google Analytics to help analyze how users use our site. This tool uses cookies to collect standard internet log information.

Your Privacy Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights including:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: At any time where we rely on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@binsaeed.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on our website with the effective date.

Significant changes will be communicated through email or prominent website notices.
